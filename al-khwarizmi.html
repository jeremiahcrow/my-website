Early Life & Education

Al-Khwarizmi, born around 780 CE in Khwarizm (modern-day Uzbekistan), lived during the Islamic Golden Age. He worked in Baghdad at the House of Wisdom, a great center for science, mathematics, and translation.

Through his exposure to Greek, Indian, and Persian works, he built upon earlier knowledge and created groundbreaking mathematical texts that influenced scholars for centuries.
