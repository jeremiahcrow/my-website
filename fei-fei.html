Early Life & Education

Born in Beijing in 1976, Fei-Fei Li immigrated to the U.S. as a teenager. She studied physics at Princeton and later earned a Ph.D. in electrical engineering from Caltech.

Major Contributions

Li co-created the ImageNet dataset, which enabled breakthroughs in deep learning. Her work made large-scale image recognition possible and fueled the deep learning revolution.

AI Foundations

Her research expanded the field of computer vision, connecting neuroscience and AI to help machines understand visual data like humans do.
